## Privacy and Cookies Policy

Welcome to **rslive.ru**. Your privacy and security when using our site is very important to us. In this policy, we explain what data we collect, how we use it and what rights you have.

### 1. Collection and use of personal data

We collect and process only the information that you voluntarily provide when using our site, namely:
* **Name** and **email address** when registering;
* **Contact information** when filling out forms on the site;
* Information that you decide to provide in public areas, such as comments or forums.

This data is used to:
* Maintain the site and provide you with access to its features;
* Improve our site and the services we provide;
* Respond to your questions and requests.

### 2. Cookies

We use cookies to improve user experience and website performance. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website.

#### Why do we use cookies?
* To authenticate users and maintain sessions;
* To collect anonymous statistics about website visits in order to improve its structure and content;
* To provide a more personalized experience on the website.

#### How to manage cookies?
You can change your browser settings to delete cookies or prevent them from being saved. Please note that disabling cookies may limit some of the site's functionality.

### 3. Disclosure of information to third parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except in the following cases:
* When it is necessary to comply with the law or regulatory requirements;
* To protect the rights, property, or safety of the **rslive.ru** website and its users.

### 4. Data protection

We take all reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or destruction. However, complete data security cannot be guaranteed on the Internet.

### 5. Your rights

You have the right to:
* Request access to your data;
* Correct inaccurate data;
* Request deletion of data (in cases provided by law);
* Object to data processing.

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the contact details provided on the website.

### 6. Changes to the privacy policy

We reserve the right to change this Policy at any time. We will notify you of changes by updating this page. We recommend that you periodically review our policy to familiarize yourself with the latest changes.

### Contacts

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or how we process your data, please contact us via the contact information located at **rslive.ru**.
